Origin Story

I grew up in a small village in Botswana,
never quite certain how far
a life like mine could reach.

Today, I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, economic opportunity, and human dignity — exploring how technology can expand who gets to dream, create, and lead.

This space is both a record of my journey and an invitation to imagine your own. If you come from a place the world does not expect much from, it can be hard to believe your ideas belong here.

They do.
