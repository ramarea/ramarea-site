Stanford

Stanford! Never has this young man been in a place filled with so much privilege and so much opportunity. Upon arrival, he was suddenly faced with new identities — and learned to navigate them all with grace.

He spent summers in Sri Lanka studying Community Health, and as he looks forward, he can’t help but marvel at the story of the kid from the village who made it this far.

Life in Decades

If I were to ever follow through on my plans to write my autobiography, I would have the following structure:

1994–2000

Genesis

The very first 6 years — the period I know least about, yet which shaped everything that followed.

2001–2012

Socialisis

Childhood into adolescence. The journey from a small village in Botswana into the wider world.

2013–2020

Metamorphosis

Transition into adulthood away from home — Costa Rica, Stanford, and everything in between.

2021–2030

Symbiosis

I am in the middle of living these years — exploring what it means to give back as much as I have received.
